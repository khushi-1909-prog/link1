Style Tips for Every Look

By Zyntho Style Experts

Kurtis are versatile for every occasion, and the right footwear enhances your overall look. Whether casual, festive, ethnic, or formal, choosing the right pair elevates comfort and style.

Casual Everyday Wear

  • Comfortable flats
  • Simple sandals
  • Slip-ons

Office & Professional Look

  • Closed-toe flats
  • Block heels
  • Kitten heels

College & Youthful Styling

  • Sneakers
  • Sporty slip-ons
  • Flat sandals

Festive & Ethnic Wear

  • Traditional juttis
  • Embellished flats
  • Heeled sandals

Wedding & Party Looks

  • Block heels
  • Metallic heels
  • Stone-work sandals

Winter Kurti Styling

  • Boots
  • Closed loafers
  • Warm sneakers
